Build a complete Villa Owner portal at /dashboard/owner
with the same elegant beige and gold theme.

This portal is for villa owners — they can ONLY see 
their own villa's data. No income data visible to 
villa managers.

1. TOP NAVIGATION:
   - "Staya Management" logo left
   - Current date center
   - Owner name + Logout right

2. LEFT SIDEBAR:
   - Dashboard (home)
   - My Villa (building)
   - Bookings (calendar) — view only
   - Revenue & Reports (chart)
   - Monthly Statements (document)
   - Petty Cash (wallet) — totals only, no amounts
   - Maintenance (tools) — status only
   - Settings (gear)

3. OWNER DASHBOARD — main page:
   - Welcome message: "Welcome back, [Owner Name]"
   - Villa name and location as subtitle
   - 4 STAT CARDS:
     * Revenue This Month (from payments table)
     * Occupancy Rate % (from bookings)
     * Upcoming Bookings (next 30 days)
     * Pending Maintenance Issues (count only)
   
   - REVENUE CHART:
     * Bar chart showing last 6 months revenue
     * Fetch from payments table for their villa only
   
   - UPCOMING BOOKINGS TABLE:
     * Check-in, Check-out, Nights, Amount, OTA, Status
     * Next 5 bookings for their villa only
     * NO guest personal details (just initials)
   
   - PETTY CASH SUMMARY:
     * Show total petty cash spent this month
     * Show by category (cleaning, maintenance, etc)
     * NO individual amounts — totals only
   
   - MAINTENANCE STATUS:
     * List open maintenance issues
     * Show: Title, Category, Priority, Status
     * NO costs visible

4. MY VILLA PAGE at /dashboard/owner/villa:
   - Villa details (read only — cannot edit)
   - Photo gallery placeholder
   - Seasonal rates (read only)
   - OTA channels connected
   - Amenities list

5. REVENUE & REPORTS at /dashboard/owner/revenue:
   - Monthly revenue breakdown
   - Management fee deduction (20%)
   - Petty cash deductions
   - Maintenance cost deductions
   - NET payout amount in gold
   - Filter by month/year
   - "Download Report" button (PDF coming later)

6. MONTHLY STATEMENTS at /dashboard/owner/statements:
   - List of all monthly statements
   - Month, Year, Gross Revenue, Deductions, Net Payout
   - Status badge: Draft / Sent / Paid
   - View button for each statement
