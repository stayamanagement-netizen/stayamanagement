Build the Villa Owner portal at /dashboard/owner
with the same elegant beige and gold theme.

IMPORTANT: Villa owners can ONLY see their own 
villa data. They cannot see income of other villas
or any villa manager data.

1. OWNER DASHBOARD (/dashboard/owner):
   - Top nav: "Staya Management" logo, date, 
     owner name + Sign Out button
   - Left sidebar:
     * Dashboard
     * My Villa
     * Bookings (view only)
     * Revenue & Reports
     * Monthly Statements
     * Petty Cash (totals only)
     * Maintenance (status only)
     * Settings

   - Welcome: "Welcome back, [Owner Name]"
   - Villa name and location as subtitle
   
   - 4 STAT CARDS:
     * Revenue This Month
     * Occupancy Rate %
     * Upcoming Bookings (next 30 days)
     * Open Maintenance Issues

   - REVENUE CHART:
     * Bar chart — last 6 months
     * Fetch from payments table for their villa

   - UPCOMING BOOKINGS TABLE:
     * Check-in, Check-out, Nights, Amount, 
       OTA channel, Status
     * NO guest personal details shown
     * Only their villa's bookings

   - PETTY CASH SUMMARY:
     * Total spent this month by category
     * NO individual line items — totals only

   - MAINTENANCE STATUS:
     * Open issues list
     * Title, Category, Priority, Status
     * NO costs shown to owner

2. MY VILLA PAGE (/dashboard/owner/villa):
   - Villa details (READ ONLY — cannot edit)
   - Villa name, location, bedrooms, capacity
   - Seasonal rates (read only)
   - OTA channels connected
   - Spare bed availability

3. REVENUE PAGE (/dashboard/owner/revenue):
   - Monthly revenue breakdown
   - Gross revenue
   - Management fee (20%) deducted
   - Petty cash deducted
   - Maintenance costs deducted
   - NET payout in gold — large and prominent
   - Filter by month and year
   - "Download Report" button (placeholder)

4. STATEMENTS PAGE (/dashboard/owner/statements):
   - List of monthly statements
   - Month, Gross, Deductions, Net, Status
   - Status: Draft / Sent / Paid
   - View button per statement

   - Can ONLY see their own villa data via villa_owners table
